DUST COVERS

Dave McKean

Comments and notes by Dave McKean and Neil Gaiman

Collects the covers of all published Sandman issues as well as interior illustrations for the collections and previously unpublished work

Dave McKean's beautiful covers are all collected here, on high quality paper. Both Neil and Dave tell about the creation of the covers, and while doing so, they talk about Sandman. After reading the small print beside the illustrations, you will know that Dave McKean really likes fish, that the A Midsummer Night's Dream issue was used in a murder, and that the world has never been the same since Dave McKean started using a computer.

Dust Covers also contains The Last Sandman Story, written by Neil Gaiman and illustrated by Dave McKean. I would say that it is a beautiful little piece of fiction, but according to Neil I might be wrong during the night. However, it is a beautiful little piece.

Published in England by Titan Books, both in hardback and paperback format.
